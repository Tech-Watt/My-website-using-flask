Computer Vision and AI Zone

Computer vision is a field of artificial intelligence and computer science that focuses on enabling computers to interpret and understand digital images and videos. It involves the development of algorithms and techniques to analyze, process, and extract useful information from digital images and videos, such as object recognition, face detection, tracking, and image restoration.Computer vision has many practical applications, including autonomous vehicles and many more.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that involves the development of computer systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and language translation. AI systems use machine learning algorithms to analyze data, learn from patterns, and make predictions or decisions based on the learned patterns. AI is the future of humanity.
