Computer Vision and AI Zone


Computer vision is a technology that enables computers to interpret and understand visual data, such as images and videos, using algorithms and techniques to extract information. It has applications in various fields, from image processing to self-driving cars and robotics. It is a good field you should consider learning now.


AI, or artificial intelligence, refers to computer systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, using algorithms and models to learn from data and improve their performance over time. It has applications in various fields, from virtual assistants and chatbots to autonomous vehicles and healthcare.
